Young Samurai: The Way of the Sword

Summary

One year in Japan and he is already in a deep mess

Masamoto has announced that the Circle of Three, an ancient ritual to test the skill, courage, and spirit to the limit, will take place. Now Jack Fletcher needs to prepare for the Circle of Three while keeping an eye out for his fellow students, Kazuki and his gang. But those are the least of his problems.

Dragon Eye is still out there, his most deadly rival. He will not stop at anything to retrieve the rutter, the most powerful logbook in the world. Can Jack master the Way of the Sword and survive a fight to the death?
